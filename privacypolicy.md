# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 24, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Little Helper bot (the "Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use it on Discord.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information
- **User IDs and usernames** - to execute commands and perform moderation actions
- **Message content** - temporarily to detect banned links and enforce rules
- **Channel information** - to identify where commands are used
- **Guild (server) information** - to apply settings per-server
- **User roles and permissions** - to determine who can use admin commands

### 1.2 Information You Provide
- **Banned links** - when you add links to the blocklist
- **Configuration data** - server preferences and settings

### 1.3 Information NOT Collected
- We do NOT store message content beyond detection
- We do NOT track DMs or private conversations
- We do NOT sell or share personal data with third parties

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:
- **Moderation** - enforcing server rules, timeouts, bans, kicks
- **Link detection** - blocking malicious URLs across servers
- **Command execution** - running commands you request
- **Logging** - deleted messages are logged for security purposes

## 3. Data Retention

- **Message logs** - stored for 20 most recent deleted messages, deleted after 3 days
- **Banned links** - retained indefinitely (you can remove them)
- **Admin list** - retained until removed
- **Bot logs** - stored for 3 days then auto-deleted

## 4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Async file locking to prevent concurrent data corruption
- Automatic log rotation and cleanup
- Command permission verification
- Mod immunity from auto-actions

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner
- Delete banned links you added
- Ask what data is stored about you

## 6. Third-Party Services

This Bot uses:
- **Discord API** - to interact with Discord servers
- **Your server's hosting** - to store configuration files

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
